## <SlGraduation style={{ display: 'inline', marginRight: '0.5rem', verticalAlign: 'middle' }} /> Who is this for?

### Platform Teams

You're responsible for frontend infrastructure and want to standardize development across your organization. 1fe helps you provide consistent tooling, governance, and shared utilities while enabling teams to work independently.

### Frontend Developers

You're building user experiences and want to focus on creating great widgets without worrying about build systems, deployment pipelines, or platform utilities. 1fe provides the foundation so you can concentrate on your application logic.

### Technical Leaders

You're evaluating micro-frontend solutions and need to understand how 1fe compares to alternatives, what it provides out of the box, and how it fits into your organization's architecture.
